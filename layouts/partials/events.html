Innovation Summit

BCUCSB collaborated with UCSB Innovation Council and other campus organizations to put together a one-day campus event (November 17, 2018) designed to foster and harness creative thinking and develop a innovative business model to improve problems found in global categories such as energy, sustainability, income inequality, digital security, and health. At the event, BCUCSB members explained real-world blockchain applications and also encouraged students to explore incorporating blockchain models in their creative business ideas. On a speaker panel of industry leaders, we interviewed Robert Greenfield, the Technical Director at Consensys, which is the leading firm within the Blockchain and Cryptocurrency markets.
